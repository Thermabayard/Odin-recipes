Tacos

Description

These easy and flavorful tacos are perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions. Simmer until thickened.
  3. Fill the taco shells with the beef mixture.
  4. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Serve immediately.

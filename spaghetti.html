Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe is simple and delicious, perfect for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the pan and heat through.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce and top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Serve hot.

Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe includes layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking ground beef with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce and seasonings.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
